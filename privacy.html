Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Quit Fap Pal ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Quit Fap Pal mobile application.

The short version: We don't collect your personal data. Your recovery journey is private, and we intend to keep it that way.

Information We DON'T Collect

Quit Fap Pal is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identification
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your contacts or phone data
  • Your browsing history
  • Your streak data, journal entries, or check-ins
  • Any health or recovery information

Local Data Storage

All your data in Quit Fap Pal is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Your streak count and history
  • Daily check-in records
  • Journal entries
  • Urge logs and coping tool usage
  • Milestone achievements
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties. If you delete the app, all this data is permanently deleted from your device.

AI Coach Feature

When you use the AI Coach feature, your messages are sent to our AI service provider (Google Gemini) to generate responses. Important notes about this:

  • Messages are processed in real-time and are not stored by us
  • We do not link your messages to any personal identity
  • The AI provider may process messages according to their privacy policy
  • You can use the app fully without using the AI Coach feature

Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated analytics to improve the app experience. This includes:

  • App crashes and error reports (no personal data)
  • General usage statistics (which features are used most)
  • Device type and iOS version (for compatibility)

This data is completely anonymous and cannot be used to identify you or your recovery journey.

Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to external content (like educational videos on YouTube). When you access these external services, their own privacy policies apply. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with.

Data Security

Since your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security features. We recommend:

  • Using a strong device passcode
  • Enabling Face ID or Touch ID
  • Using the app's built-in PIN lock feature
  • Keeping your iOS updated

Children's Privacy

Quit Fap Pal is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17. If you are under 17, please do not use this app without parental guidance.

Data Export and Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: You can export your data from the app's Settings menu
  • Deletion: You can delete all your data from Settings, or simply delete the app

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Through the app's Contact feature
  • Via our website at quitfappal.com

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access your data (it's all on your device!)
  • The right to delete your data (available in Settings)
  • The right to data portability (export feature available)

Since we don't collect personal data, most data protection regulations don't apply to our service. However, we believe in these principles and have built our app to give you full control.
