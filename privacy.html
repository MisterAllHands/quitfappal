Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Quit Fap Pal ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Quit Fap Pal mobile application.

The short version: Your recovery journey data stays on your device. We collect minimal data for authentication and subscriptions, and send contextual information to our AI provider only when you use the AI Coach feature.

Information We Collect

Authentication Data

Quit Fap Pal uses Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In for authentication. When you sign in, we receive:

  • A unique user identifier
  • Your email address (if provided by your authentication provider)
  • Your display name (if provided)
  • Your profile photo URL (if available)

We use this information solely to identify your account and enable data sync. We do not share this information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Subscription Data

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. When you subscribe, RevenueCat collects:

  • Purchase history and transaction records
  • Subscription status and renewal information
  • An anonymous identifier linked to your account

This data is necessary to provide you with premium features and manage your subscription. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Information We DON'T Collect

Quit Fap Pal is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do NOT collect:

  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your contacts or phone data
  • Your browsing history
  • Device advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Any health data from HealthKit

Your Recovery Data

Your recovery data is stored locally on your device and includes:

  • Your streak count and history
  • Daily check-in records (mood, notes, coping strategies)
  • Journal entries
  • Urge logs and coping tool usage
  • Milestone achievements
  • Onboarding responses (motivations, triggers, personal vision)
  • AI-generated insights and weekly summaries
  • Conversation history with the AI Coach
  • App preferences and settings

This data is stored securely on your device and associated with your account. You can delete all your data at any time from the app settings.

AI Coach Feature

When you use the AI Coach feature, information is sent to our AI service provider (Google Gemini) to generate personalized responses. This includes:

  • Your messages to the coach
  • Your current recovery statistics (streak length, clean days, goal progress)
  • Today's activity summary (check-ins completed, urges logged, tools used)
  • Your motivations and personal vision (from onboarding)
  • Self-reported triggers and high-risk times
  • Detected behavioral patterns from your journey data
  • Insights extracted from your journal entries
  • Current time of day and whether you're in a high-risk period

Important notes about AI data:

  • No personally identifiable information (name, email) is sent to the AI
  • This context helps the AI provide relevant, personalized support
  • Messages are processed according to Google's Privacy Policy
  • You can use the app fully without using the AI Coach feature

Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated analytics to improve the app experience. This includes:

  • App crashes and error reports (no personal data)
  • General usage statistics (which features are used most)
  • Device type and iOS version (for compatibility)

This data is completely anonymous and cannot be used to identify you or your recovery journey.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure sign-in (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Gemini: For AI Coach responses (Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management (Privacy Policy)

The app may also contain links to external content (like educational videos on YouTube). When you access these external services, their own privacy policies apply.

Data Security

Your recovery data is stored locally on your device. Its security depends on your device's security features. We recommend:

  • Using a strong device passcode
  • Enabling Face ID or Touch ID
  • Using the app's built-in PIN lock feature
  • Keeping your iOS updated

Children's Privacy

Quit Fap Pal is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17. If you are under 17, please do not use this app without parental guidance.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@quitfappal.com
  • Through the app's Contact feature

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access your data (available in Settings)
  • The right to delete your data (available in Settings)
  • The right to data portability (export feature available)
  • The right to opt out of data collection (don't use AI Coach)

For California residents: We do not sell your personal information. You may request deletion of your data at any time through the app settings.
